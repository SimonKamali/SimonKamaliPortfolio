Case

Innovation in urban sustainability.
Gothenburg is looking for new innovative ways to improve urban sustainability, for a better, greener future, with focus on re-usability and changing the behaviour of the people for the better.

The app

Introducing 'RideShare,' our innovative app that revolutionizes urban living for the eco-conscious city dweller. At the core of this groundbreaking solution is the idea that urban sustainability is achievable through smarter, more efficient transportation choices.

'RideShare' aims to make a substantial impact by transforming the way residents access and utilize transportation within their city. Picture this:
in most apartment complexes, you'll find a fleet of electric cars, bicycles, and electric mopeds readily available for residents to rent. With 'RideShare,' residents have the opportunity to ditch their personal vehicles, reducing traffic congestion and emissions, while enjoying the flexibility of renting a sustainable mode of transportation whenever they need it. Whether it's a quick trip to the grocery store, a commute to work, or a weekend adventure, 'RideShare' provides a convenient, cost-effective, and eco-friendly solution. By encouraging vehicle sharing, we're not just reducing the number of cars on the road, but also promoting cleaner, greener transportation options.

'RideShare' is a game-changer in the journey toward urban sustainability, making cities cleaner, more livable, and environmentally responsible for all residents.

Style sheet

Fonts


In our app's design, we incorporated Bubbleboddy Light for the logo and headers. This sleek, rounded font exudes a clean and modern aesthetic, creating a captivating first impression.

For the body text, we embraced IBM Plex Sans Extra Light, chosen for its clarity and readability. This combination strikes a perfect balance between style and substance, ensuring an engaging and professional user experience. Our font selection embodies the essence of our app: visually appealing and user-friendly.



Colours and styling



In our app's design, we've embraced a colour scheme that embodies modernity, a futuristic edge, sleek lines, and a clean, polished look. The pitch-black background, subtly adorned with a calming blue gradient, sets the stage for a sleek and immersive user experience. Our choice of aqua gradients for text highlights and buttons exudes a futuristic vibe, reminiscent of neon lights, giving the app a distinctively modern and stylish feel. The colours work harmoniously to create a visually captivating interface, striking the perfect balance between a clean, polished appearance and a sleek, futuristic aesthetic.
